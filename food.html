Whether it’s fine restaurants, pub food, a light snack in a bar or late night entertainment Peterborough has a great selection of places to eat and drink. Taste from tables around the world with a spicy Mexican, a fragrant Thai, a creamy curry, stylish Italian or hearty English - we can offer them all. For a romantic treat, try one of our floating restaurants down by the river or if you’re off to the theatre, catch a pre-performance supper at Resit! Vegan Kitchen at the Key Theatre. And if you’re looking for the tastiest street food then be sure to pay the City Market a visit.
